Tips for shipping

A) Please take a attention that APO/FPO & PO Boxes are not accepted.

B) Package to Canada and the United Kingdom may be paid for clearance customs tax when you pick it up. For more remote areas in Canada, please allow an additional 1-2 days for delivery.

C)  We are doing our best to make the promised time, please understand our warehouse are working only on business days, so please remember to enjoy the sunshine even there's a day or two difference in the ETA, and by the way, we will inform you proactively if something is wrong.

D) Phone number is required for delivery. Leave correct email address in your order so that order confirmation and shipping confirmation will be sent.

E) Your order can be tracked in details only when it arrives in US Facility. Kindly allow 2-3 days of shipment to US, even possible " not found ". Thanks for your patience. Should you have any question, please reach out to our service center: service@cupshe.zendesk.com.
